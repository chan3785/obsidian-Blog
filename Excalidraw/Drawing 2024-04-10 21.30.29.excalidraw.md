The target detection algorithm is
    X > xT or Y > 0 target present
    X <= xT or Y <= 0 target absent (only noise received)
clearly xT should depend on the intensity of noise
                        
- False alarm: when the target is absent, but noise
               exceeds xT
- Miss detection: when the target is present, but the target
                 signal is too weak to exceed xT
